Welcome to the Floating Islands

You have arrived at the sacred islands of the Métis people—floating platforms that hold the knowledge and traditions of a nation born from the meeting of cultures.

Each island carries teachings about language, craft, medicine, and the deep connections that bind all things together. Visit the Elders, explore the gathering fires, and discover the rich heritage of the Métis.

Your Journey

  • Visit 5 Elder Islands — each has wisdom to share and a question to test your learning
  • Explore 6 Experience Islands — discover interactive teachings about Métis culture
  • Find 6 Cultural Islands — medicine gardens, fishing, farming, and more
